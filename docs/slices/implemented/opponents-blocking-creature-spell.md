# Opponent's Blocking Creature Spell

## Summary

Allow a supported targeted instant to require a creature that is blocking and controlled by an opponent of the acting player or controller.

## Scope

- add an explicit closed legal-target rule for `opponent-controlled blocking creature`
- prove the rule in the current post-blockers active-priority window
- reject combat creatures that fail the opponent-or-blocking side of the rule

## Out Of Scope

- richer opponent-relative blocking restrictions such as controller-choice clauses or multiple targets
