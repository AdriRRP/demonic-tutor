# Resolve Targeted Attacking Creature Damage

## Summary

Allow a supported `AttackingCreature` damage instant to resolve after attackers are declared, mark lethal damage on the attacker, and let the shared SBA corridor destroy it.

## Scope

- resolve damage to the attacking creature target
- rely on the shared state-based-action review for lethal destruction

## Out Of Scope

- richer combat redirection or prevention effects
