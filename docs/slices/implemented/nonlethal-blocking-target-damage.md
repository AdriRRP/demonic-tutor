# Nonlethal Blocking Target Damage

## Summary

Allow a supported `BlockingCreature` damage spell to mark nonlethal damage and leave the blocking creature alive in the current combat state.

## Scope

- resolve nonlethal damage onto the blocking creature target
- keep the target on the battlefield when SBA do not find lethal damage
- preserve the blocking state while combat is still in progress

## Out Of Scope

- richer combat consequences beyond marked damage and continued blocking status
