# Cast Flash Enchantment After Blockers

## Status

Implemented

## Scope

- an enchantment card with minimal `Flash`-like support may be cast by the active player after blockers are declared
- the cast happens in the post-blockers open priority window
- on resolution the enchantment enters the battlefield

## Out Of Scope

- broader non-creature `Flash`
- triggered abilities tied to the enchantment entering
