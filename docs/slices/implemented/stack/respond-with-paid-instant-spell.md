# Respond With Paid Instant Spell

## Status

Implemented

## Scope

- the non-active player may respond on an open stack by first tapping a land for mana
- the produced mana may be spent immediately to cast an instant response with mana cost `1`
- the response still resolves through the ordinary two-pass stack corridor

## Out Of Scope

- colored mana
- mana abilities as a separate rules engine
- costs above the currently exercised generic subset
