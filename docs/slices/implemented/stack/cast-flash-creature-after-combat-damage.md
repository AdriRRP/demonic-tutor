# Cast Flash Creature After Combat Damage

## Summary

Allow a supported creature card with explicit open-priority casting permission to be cast in the `EndOfCombat` priority window that reopens after combat damage resolves.

## Scope

- reuse the minimal `Flash`-like casting rule already supported on card faces
- prove the behavior after combat damage resolves while the game remains active

## Out Of Scope

- richer post-combat stack interactions beyond the supported combat corridor
