# Nonlethal Controlled Blocking Target Damage

## Summary

Allow a supported `controlled blocking creature` damage spell to mark nonlethal damage on the chosen blocker while keeping that creature in combat.

## Scope

- resolve nonlethal damage onto the controlled blocking target
- leave the blocker alive on the battlefield
- preserve the blocker's blocking state after resolution

## Out Of Scope

- broader combat state changes beyond the current supported damage and SBA corridor
