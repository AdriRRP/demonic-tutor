# Target Opponents Creature In First Main

## Status

Implemented

## Scope

- the targeted-spell subset supports a spell that must target a creature controlled by an opponent of the acting player
- the supported scenario is exercised outside combat in `FirstMain`
- the spell enters the stack with the chosen opponent-controlled creature target preserved

## Out Of Scope

- rejection of self-controlled creatures for this rule family
- full resolution coverage for this rule family
- multiplayer opponent sets
