# Cast Targeted Blocking Creature Spell After Blockers

## Summary

Allow the current priority holder to cast a supported `BlockingCreature` instant in the post-blockers combat window.

## Scope

- reuse the explicit `BlockingCreature` legal-target rule
- prove the behavior in the current post-blockers priority window
- keep the spell in the existing targeted instant subset

## Out Of Scope

- lethal or nonlethal damage consequences for the blocking creature
