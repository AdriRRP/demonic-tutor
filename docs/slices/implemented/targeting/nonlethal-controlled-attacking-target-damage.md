# Nonlethal Controlled Attacking Target Damage

## Summary

Allow a supported `controlled attacking creature` damage spell to mark nonlethal damage on the chosen attacker while keeping that creature in combat.

## Scope

- resolve nonlethal damage onto the controlled attacking target
- leave the attacker alive on the battlefield
- preserve the attacker's attacking state after resolution

## Out Of Scope

- broader combat state changes beyond the current supported damage and SBA corridor
