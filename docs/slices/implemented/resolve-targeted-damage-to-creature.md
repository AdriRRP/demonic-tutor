# Slice Name

ResolveTargetedDamageToCreature

---

## Goal

Apply marked damage to a targeted creature during spell resolution.

---

## Supported Behavior

- a supported targeted instant may mark damage on a creature target
- the damage is applied during stack resolution, not at cast time
- player life does not change when the target is a creature

---

## Rules Reference

- 120
- 608.2

---

## Rules Support Statement

When a supported targeted instant resolves with a creature target, the spell marks damage on that creature during resolution.
