# Opponent's Attacking Creature Spell

## Summary

Allow a supported targeted instant to require a creature that is attacking and controlled by an opponent of the acting player or controller.

## Scope

- add an explicit closed legal-target rule for `opponent-controlled attacking creature`
- prove the rule in the post-attackers response window for the defending player
- reject creatures that fail the opponent-or-attacking side of the rule

## Out Of Scope

- richer opponent-relative targeting such as `attacking creature you don't control unless ...`
